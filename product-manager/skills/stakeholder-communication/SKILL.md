# stakeholder-communication

Use this skill to communicate product status with high signal and low noise.

## Workflow

1. Start with outcomes, not activity.
2. Compare progress against committed plan.
3. Surface decisions, risks, and asks early.
4. Match detail level to stakeholder audience.
5. End with next steps and accountability.

## Quality bar

- Message is concise and decision-ready.
- No ambiguity on ownership.
- No hidden risks.
