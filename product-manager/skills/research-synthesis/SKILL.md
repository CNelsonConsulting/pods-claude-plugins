# research-synthesis

Use this skill to convert raw customer research into decisions.

## Workflow

1. Cluster raw evidence by user need/theme.
2. Separate observed facts from interpretation.
3. Quantify confidence and representativeness.
4. Map insights to product opportunities.
5. Recommend experiments or feature direction.

## Quality bar

- Insights are evidence-backed.
- Confidence is explicit.
- Recommended actions are testable.
