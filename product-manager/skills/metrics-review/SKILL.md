# metrics-review

Use this skill to interpret product metrics and drive next actions.

## Workflow

1. Validate KPI definitions and data freshness.
2. Compare actuals against targets and cohorts.
3. Identify likely causal drivers.
4. Propose interventions with expected impact.
5. Define follow-up checks and owners.

## Quality bar

- Metrics interpretations avoid over-claiming.
- Actions are prioritized by impact and effort.
- Follow-up is measurable.
