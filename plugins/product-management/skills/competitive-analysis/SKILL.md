# competitive-analysis

Use this skill to evaluate competitors without losing focus on user value.

## Workflow

1. Define comparison criteria tied to user jobs-to-be-done.
2. Summarize competitor strengths and weaknesses.
3. Identify meaningful gaps and parity needs.
4. Assess strategic impact on your roadmap.
5. Recommend positioning and product responses.

## Quality bar

- Analysis is specific, not generic.
- Recommendations are actionable.
- Differentiation is tied to user outcomes.
