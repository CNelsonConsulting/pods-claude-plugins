# spec-writing

Use this skill to produce clear, testable product specs.

## Workflow

1. Clarify the user problem and business objective.
2. Define goals, non-goals, and success criteria.
3. Convert needs into concrete requirements.
4. Enumerate edge cases and dependencies.
5. Close with risks and unresolved questions.

## Quality bar

- Requirements are unambiguous and measurable.
- Scope is explicit.
- Tradeoffs are documented.
