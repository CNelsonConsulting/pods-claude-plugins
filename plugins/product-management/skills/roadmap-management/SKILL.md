# roadmap-management

Use this skill to create realistic, priority-driven roadmap updates.

## Workflow

1. Gather current initiative status.
2. Re-rank priorities against goals and constraints.
3. Highlight sequencing dependencies.
4. Call out risk and confidence by initiative.
5. Recommend decisions with deadline owners.

## Quality bar

- Priorities have explicit rationale.
- Timeline assumptions are clear.
- Risks are paired with mitigation.
